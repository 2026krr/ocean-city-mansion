EST. 1898 · OCEAN CITY, NJ

Where Heritage Meets the Shore

Built in 1898 and listed on the National Register of Historic Places, Ocean City Mansion stands as a testament to French Modern architecture on the Jersey Shore. Completely reimagined for 2026, every detail honors the building's Victorian character while embracing the warmth and ease of modern coastal living. Twelve individually designed rooms. A wraparound porch made for slow mornings. Steps from the beach and boardwalk.
