Why We Partner with Creators

Your Story, Our Shore

Ocean City Mansion was built for storytelling. From the restored Victorian architecture to the wraparound porch at golden hour, every corner of this property is designed to inspire. We partner with content creators, photographers, travel writers, and influencers who share our passion for the coast and can bring the Ocean City Mansion experience to life for their audiences.

We are not chasing follower counts. We are looking for genuine connection, quality content, and creators whose aesthetic and audience align with what we are building here in Ocean City.

Complimentary Stays

Experience Ocean City Mansion as a guest — rooms, suites, or apartments tailored to your content needs.

Creative Freedom

We believe in authentic storytelling. Capture Ocean City Mansion your way — no scripts, no shot lists.

Long-Term Relationships

We value ongoing partnerships over one-off posts. Great collaborations grow over time.

What We Look For

  • Authentic, high-quality visual content that tells a story
  • An engaged audience — we care more about engagement than follower count
  • Alignment with the Ocean City Mansion aesthetic: Victorian, design-forward, coastal ease
  • Travel, lifestyle, food, family, or design-focused content creators
  • Creators based in the Northeast US or with audiences in our target markets (Philadelphia, New York, Baltimore, Washington DC)
  • Professional communication and ability to deliver on agreed timelines
