Introduction

Ocean City Mansion ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in each email.
